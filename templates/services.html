Our Products and Services

This is the core Sacco function that enables members to save and be able to access credit facilities. We encourage our members to save for the future. Adherence to the 1/3 government basic salary rule applies to all loan products. A member can only qualify for one loan product at a time. All loans are disbursed through the members' accounts. The following are our products and services:

Entry Level Loans

Normal Loan
  • Granted at three times the member's deposit
  • Maximum repayment period is 36 months
  • Interest rate is 1% p.m on reducing balance
Branch Loan
  • It caters for emergency needs
  • Maximum repayment period is 18 months
  • Interest rate is 1% p.m on reducing balance
Dividends Advance
  • To qualify the advance should not exceed 5% of total deposit as at 31st Dec 2020

Premium Loans

Supersaver Loan
  • Granted at four times the member's deposit
  • Applies to members with deposits 200,000/- and above
  • Maximum repayment period is 48 months
  • Interest rate is 1% p.m on reducing balance
Golden Loan
  • Interest rate is 1% p.m on reducing balance
  • Maximum repayment period is 72 months (6 years)
  • Minimum deposit is Ksh. 250,000
  • Interest rate is 1.25% p.m (15% p.a) on amortization
  • Monthly deposit will be 10% of basic salary granted at 4 times member's deposits
Super Loan
  • Minimum loan amount is Ksh.300,000
  • Maximum repayment period is 60 months
  • Interest rate is 1.17% pm on amortization

Special Loans

Express Loan
  • Tailored for members with urgent financial needs
  • Maximum processing period is 2 days
  • Interest rate is 1% p.m on reducing balance
Msingi Loan
  • Granted for newly recruited members
  • Maximum repayment period is 48 months
  • Payable interest of 1% p.m on reducing balance
  • Processing fee of Ksh.500 is applicable
  • Loan is granted once in membership life
